Iterate - Company Values

CORE VALUES:
1. Ownership - We take full responsibility for our work and outcomes. We own our mistakes and learn from them.
2. Accountability - We are transparent about our work, admit when we're wrong, and hold ourselves to high standards.
3. Curiosity - We ask questions, seek to understand deeply, and continuously learn new things.
4. Teamwork - We collaborate effectively, support our teammates, and value collective success over individual achievement.
5. Humility - We acknowledge our limitations, are open to feedback, and recognize that we don't know everything.
6. Communication Clarity - We explain things clearly, think structured, and ensure everyone understands.
7. Growth Mindset - We embrace challenges, see failures as learning opportunities, and adapt quickly.
8. Transparency - We are honest, direct, and share information openly with our team.

WHAT WE VALUE:
- Proactive problem-solving
- Learning from mistakes
- Supporting team members
- Clear and honest communication
- Continuous improvement

WHAT WE AVOID:
- Blame-shifting
- Making excuses
- Arrogance or dismissive behavior
- Avoiding responsibility
- Toxic or negative attitudes
